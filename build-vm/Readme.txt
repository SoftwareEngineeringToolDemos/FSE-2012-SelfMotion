1. Open eclipse

2. Click on the Android Virtual Device Manager

3. Click on the device called "AndroidDevice" and click on the "Start" button. In the window that pops up, click "Launch"

4. After the device has finished loading click on the run button to install the app on the emulator

5. When the app is done installing, it will automatically launch

6. Click on "Share prices" to 'scan a barcode' to share a price of a product in the store. Since the emulator cannot scan anything, hit 'esc' to manually enter in a product code. The app will then try to find your location to compare the prices of the product.
